Frequently asked questions
Why does ChatGPT remove my formatting?
ChatGPT and most AI chat interfaces accept plain text only. Bold, colors, and headings are stripped. RetainFormat converts them into explicit tags (e.g. <Answer>, <Question>) so the AI keeps your structure.
How do I keep bold and colors when pasting into AI?
Paste your text here, click Parse formatting, assign a meaning to each style (e.g. bold = Question), then Generate text and copy. Paste that tagged output into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.
Can I use this with text from Word or Google Docs?
Yes. Paste from Word, Docs, PDF, or email. RetainFormat detects bold, italic, colors, and alignment and turns them into semantic tags for AI.
